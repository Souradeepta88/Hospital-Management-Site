🔍 How it Works

Uses a NOT EXISTS correlated subquery to find doctors who have zero visits in the past 12 months. Doctors with no history at all are also included.

NOT EXISTS subquery
📅 12-Month Window

PostgreSQL equivalent of Oracle's ADD_MONTHS(SYSDATE,-12) — uses CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '12 months' for compatibility.

CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '12 months'
⚙️ Action

These doctors should be contacted for scheduling review, status verification, or potential deactivation using P1 → Deactivate Doctor.
