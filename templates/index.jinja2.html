Advanced To Do Tasks

Five carefully crafted database operations — from simple queries to complex joins, triggers, and stored procedures. Each task reveals the full power of the Silver Care data model.

Simple Query Task S1
🔍
Active Cardiology Specialists
Retrieve the ID, name, qualification and specialization of all currently active doctors in the Cardiology department. A clean, targeted lookup that powers the specialist directory.
SELECT · WHERE · FILTER

Complex Query Task C1
📊
Patients with Total Visits & Total Amount Billed
Find every patient along with a count of their total hospital visits and the cumulative amount billed across all history records. Aggregated with JOIN, GROUP BY & HAVING.
JOIN · GROUP BY · HAVING · SUM

Complex Query Task C2
💊
Amoxicillin Prescriptions & Prescribing Doctors
Identify all patients prescribed Amoxicillin, the doctor who wrote the prescription, and the visit date. Achieved through a 4-table chain join across patients, prescriptions, doctors and medications.
4-TABLE JOIN · MEDICATION FILTER

Trigger Task T1
⚡
Auto-Validate Patient History on Insert
A BEFORE INSERT trigger on the PatientHistory table — automatically defaults visit_date to today when not provided, and raises an exception if BillAmount is negative.
TRIGGER · BEFORE INSERT · VALIDATE

Procedure Task P1
⚙️
Deactivate a Doctor by ID
A stored procedure that sets a doctor's status to Inactive using their unique ID — with built-in row-count validation to confirm whether the operation succeeded or the ID wasn't found.
PROCEDURE · UPDATE · ROW COUNT
