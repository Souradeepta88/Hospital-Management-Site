Top 10 Most-Visiting Patients & Total Spending
Ranks the top 10 patients by visit count alongside their total cumulative spend — using correlated subquery, GROUP BY and LIMIT 10.

Complex QueryTask 08
🔄
Doctor Repeat-Patient Frequency Analysis
Calculates total visits, unique patients and average visits-per-patient for every doctor — LEFT JOIN + NULLIF division guard.

Simple QueryTask 09

Patients Who Visited the Same Doctor at Least Twice
Reveals loyal patient–doctor pairs — grouped by both names, filtered with HAVING COUNT ≥ 2, sorted by repeat frequency.

High Cost Patients
