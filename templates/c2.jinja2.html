Amoxicillin Prescriptions

A 4-table chain join — patients, prescriptions, doctors and medications — to identify every patient who was prescribed Amoxicillin, who prescribed it, and when.

SQL Query4-TABLE JOIN
SELECT  p.username     AS patient_name,
        d.username     AS doctor_name,
        m.medication,
        pr.visit_date
FROM    patients p
JOIN    prescription pr  ON p.username  = pr.patient_name
JOIN    doctor d         ON pr.doctor_name = d.username
JOIN    medication m    ON pr.prescription_number = m.prescription_number
WHERE   m.medication = 'Amoxicillin'
ORDER BY pr.visit_date DESC;
-- 4 tables joined · filtered to Amoxicillin · sorted by recency
