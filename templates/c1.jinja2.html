Patients — Visits & Total
Amount Billed

Aggregates every patient's total hospital visits and cumulative billing amount across all history records, using JOIN, GROUP BY and HAVING to surface only patients with at least one visit.

SQL QueryJOIN · GROUP BY · HAVING
SELECT   p.id, p.username,
         COUNT(ph.id)        AS total_visits,
         SUM(ph.BillAmount)  AS total_spent
FROM     patients p
JOIN     patienthistory ph ON p.username = ph.patient_name
GROUP BY p.id, p.username
HAVING   COUNT(ph.id) > 0
ORDER BY total_spent DESC;
-- Ranks patients by total spend · only patients with visits included
