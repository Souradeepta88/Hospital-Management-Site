Outpatient
Admission Cost
Guard

A BEFORE INSERT trigger on the costs table that enforces a database-level rule: when a patient is marked as an outpatient (admitted = false), the admission_cost must be zero — it cannot be set to any positive value. The costs form also disables the admission cost field visually when outpatient is selected, and the display table shows "Not Applicable" for outpatient admission costs.
