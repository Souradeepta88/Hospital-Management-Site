An AFTER INSERT trigger on the costs table that automatically keeps each patient's total_cost in the patients table up-to-date — no manual aggregation needed. Also adds and backfills the total_cost column on deploy.

Fires After Insert
Every time a new row is inserted into costs, this trigger fires immediately after the insert completes.
AFTER INSERT ON costs

Accumulates Costs
Adds the new record's admission, medicine and doctor fee to the patient's running total_cost in the patients table.
UPDATE patients SET total_cost

Backfills on Deploy
Deploying also runs an UPDATE to sync existing costs data into total_cost, so historical records are accurate from day one.
